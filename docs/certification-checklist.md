## 1. SBA VetCert (SDVOSB) ⭐ APPLY FIRST

**Why:** Fastest approval (12 days). Opens 5% of all federal contracts ($31B+/year).
**Cost:** FREE
**Duration:** 3 years
**Website:** veterans.certify.sba.gov

### Requirements:
- [ ] 51%+ owned by service-disabled veteran
- [ ] Veteran holds highest officer position (Managing Member for LLC)
- [ ] Veteran manages day-to-day operations
- [ ] Any VA disability rating (even 0%)
- [ ] Active SAM.gov registration

### Documents:
- [ ] DD-214
- [ ] VA disability rating letter
- [ ] Articles of Organization
- [ ] Operating Agreement (signed, dated)
- [ ] Membership certificates
- [ ] Business licenses
- [ ] Federal tax returns (business, 3 years or all available)
- [ ] Financial statements
- [ ] Proof of U.S. citizenship
- [ ] Owner resume
- [ ] Active SAM.gov with matching UEI/EIN

### Renewal: Recertify within 90 days before expiration (every 3 years)

---

## 2. SBA WOSB / EDWOSB ⭐ APPLY SIMULTANEOUSLY WITH VETCERT

**Why:** Additional 5% federal goal. EDWOSB opens even more set-aside categories.
**Cost:** FREE
**Duration:** 3 years
**Website:** certifications.sba.gov

### WOSB Requirements:
- [ ] 51%+ owned and controlled by women U.S. citizens
- [ ] Women manage day-to-day operations

### EDWOSB Additional Requirements (apply for this too):
- [ ] Personal net worth under $850,000
- [ ] Adjusted gross income $400,000 or less (3-year average)
- [ ] Personal assets under $6.5M

### Documents (WOSB):
- [ ] Active SAM.gov registration
- [ ] Proof of citizenship (birth cert, passport, or naturalization)
- [ ] Articles of Organization + filing receipt
- [ ] Operating Agreement
- [ ] Membership certificates (front and back + next unissued)
- [ ] Business licenses
- [ ] Owner resume

### Additional Documents (EDWOSB):
- [ ] 3 years personal federal tax returns with ALL schedules
- [ ] 3 years business federal tax returns with ALL schedules
- [ ] 3 years W-2s/1099s
- [ ] SBA Form 413 (Personal Financial Statement) — for owner AND spouse
- [ ] Bank statements
- [ ] Investment account statements
- [ ] Real estate records

### Renewal: Recertify every 3 years

## 3. SBA 8(a) Business Development ⭐ MOST VALUABLE — APPLY ASAP

**Why:** Sole-source contracts up to $4.5M without competitive bidding. 9-year program. Game changer.
**Cost:** FREE
**Duration:** 9 years (4 developmental + 5 transitional). Cannot re-enter after completion.
**Website:** certify.sba.gov

### Requirements:
- [ ] 51%+ owned by socially & economically disadvantaged U.S. citizens
- [ ] African American = "presumed socially disadvantaged" (still must write narrative)
- [ ] Personal net worth under $850,000
- [ ] Adjusted gross income $400,000 or less (3-year avg)
- [ ] Personal assets under $6.5M
- [ ] Good character
- [ ] Potential for success (2 years in business recommended, but SBA can waive)

### Documents:
- [ ] **Personal Narrative of Social Disadvantage** — detailed written statement describing specific instances of bias, prejudice, or discrimination experienced (THIS IS CRITICAL — take time writing it)
- [ ] SBA Form 1010-IND (Individual Information form)
- [ ] 3 years personal federal tax returns with ALL schedules
- [ ] 3 years business federal tax returns with ALL schedules
- [ ] SBA Form 413 (Personal Financial Statement)
- [ ] Bank statements
- [ ] Investment account statements
- [ ] Real estate records / property deeds
- [ ] Articles of Organization
- [ ] Operating Agreement
- [ ] Membership certificates
- [ ] Resumes for ALL owners, officers, and key employees
- [ ] Proof of citizenship
- [ ] IRS authorization to release tax return information
- [ ] Active SAM.gov registration
- [ ] Business licenses
- [ ] Any existing contracts or revenue documentation

### Annual Review: SBA reviews eligibility annually. Must provide updated financial records.

## 4. NJ MWBE ⭐ EASY — NJ-BASED, FEE WAIVED

**Why:** Opens NJ state contracts. You're already in NJ. Fee is waived.
**Cost:** FREE (fee waived indefinitely)
**Duration:** Annual verification
**Website:** njportal.com/DOR/SBERegistry

### Requirements:
- [ ] 51%+ owned and controlled by minority/women
- [ ] U.S. citizen
- [ ] NJ resident
- [ ] Owner manages daily operations

### Documents:
- [ ] Completed online application
- [ ] Notarized attestation statement
- [ ] Proof of NJ residency
- [ ] Articles of Organization
- [ ] Operating Agreement
- [ ] Membership certificates
- [ ] Business licenses
- [ ] Tax returns
- [ ] Financial statements
- [ ] Proof of citizenship
- [ ] Proof of minority/gender status
- [ ] Owner resume

### Fast Track: Available if you already hold Port Authority, WBENC, or NYS MWBE certification
### Renewal: Annual recertification
### Contact: (609) 292-2146

## 5. NYS MWBE + NYC M/WBE + Port Authority (ONE-STOP APPLICATION)

**Why:** One application covers all three. 30% MWBE goal on NY state contracts. Port Authority 20% MBE + 10% WBE goals.
**Cost:** FREE
**Duration:** 3-5 years depending on agency
**Website:** ny.newnycontracts.com (Empire State Development)

### IMPORTANT PREREQUISITE:
- [ ] **File for Authority to Do Business in NYS** with NY Department of State (required for NJ-based LLC before applying)

### Requirements:
- [ ] 51%+ minority/women ownership
- [ ] Must have Authority to Do Business in NYS
- [ ] Personal net worth under $15M (NYS) / $3.5M (NYC)
- [ ] 1+ year in business (NYC requirement)
- [ ] For NYC: main office in NYC or surrounding counties, OR demonstrate NYC market presence

### Documents:
- [ ] Certification Affidavit (notarized)
- [ ] Personal Net Worth Affidavit (notarized)
- [ ] Personal Net Worth Worksheet
- [ ] Proof of citizenship or permanent residency
- [ ] Proof of minority group member status
- [ ] Proof of gender
- [ ] Proof of business capitalization (how you funded the business)
- [ ] Personal and business tax returns (federal and state)
- [ ] W-2s with W-3 from the business
- [ ] Bank signature cards or authorization letter
- [ ] Resumes for all owners, directors, officers, key employees
- [ ] Proof of NY State business presence
- [ ] Current lease/deed for business location
- [ ] Articles of Organization + filing receipt
- [ ] Operating Agreement
- [ ] Membership certificates
- [ ] All business licenses, permits, certifications
- [ ] Application for Authority + filing receipt (NJ-based entity)
- [ ] **Port Authority addendum** (included in NYS application)
- [ ] **NYC SBS addendum** (included in NYS application)

### NYC M/WBE Contact: mwbe@sbs.nyc.gov
### Port Authority Contact: certhelp@panynj.gov or (212) 435-7888

## 6. NYS SDVOB (NY State Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned)

**Why:** 6% goal on NY state contracts. Separate from federal SDVOSB.
**Cost:** FREE
**Duration:** Periodic recertification
**Website:** sdves.ogs.ny.gov

### Requirements:
- [ ] 51%+ owned by service-disabled veteran
- [ ] **10% or greater VA disability rating** (higher threshold than SBA's 0%)
- [ ] Veteran controls day-to-day decisions
- [ ] 300 or fewer employees
- [ ] Significant NY State business presence

### Documents:
- [ ] DD-214 and/or NG-214
- [ ] VA disability letter dated within 1 YEAR (must be 10%+ rating)
- [ ] IRS documentation verifying FEIN
- [ ] 3 years business federal tax returns
- [ ] Owner resume
- [ ] Articles of Organization
- [ ] Operating Agreement
- [ ] Proof of NY State operations

### Contact: (518) 474-2015 or sdvob@ogs.ny.gov

## 7. DBE (Disadvantaged Business Enterprise)

**Why:** Required for airport and transit contracts (JFK, EWR, NJ Transit).
**Cost:** FREE
**Duration:** Varies (currently in flux)
**Website:** njdot.dbesystem.com (apply online only — no paper)

### ⚠️ NOTE: As of October 2025, race/sex presumption of disadvantage was REMOVED. Must make individualized showing of disadvantage.

### Requirements:
- [ ] Small business per SBA standards
- [ ] 51%+ owned by socially & economically disadvantaged individual(s)
- [ ] Personal net worth under $2,047,000
- [ ] Must write individual narrative of disadvantage (no presumptions)

### Documents:
- [ ] DBE/ACDBE Uniform Certification Application (17 pages)
- [ ] Personal Net Worth Statement (all line items)
- [ ] Personal Narrative describing social disadvantage
- [ ] Government photo ID + birth certificate
- [ ] 3 years personal tax returns with ALL W-2s/1099s
- [ ] 3 years business tax returns with all schedules
- [ ] 3 years financial statements (audited or balance sheet + income statement)
- [ ] Current balance sheet
- [ ] Resumes for all owners, officers, key personnel
- [ ] Articles of Organization, Operating Agreement
- [ ] Membership certificates
- [ ] Business licenses
- [ ] Lease agreements
- [ ] Bank statements + signature cards
- [ ] Equipment list (owned or leased)
- [ ] Bonding information (if applicable)
- [ ] NJ business registration proof

### NOTE: NJDOT will conduct on-site visit and audio-recorded owner interview
### Contact: (609) 963-2051 or DOT.DBE_ESBEPrograms@dot.nj.gov

## 8. HUBZone

**Why:** Set-aside contracts for businesses in underutilized areas.
**Cost:** FREE
**Duration:** 3 years
**Website:** certifications.sba.gov
**CHECK FIRST:** maps.certify.sba.gov/hubzone/map — enter your Elizabeth NJ address

### Requirements:
- [ ] Principal office physically in a HUBZone (check map first!)
- [ ] 51%+ owned by U.S. citizens
- [ ] 35%+ of employees reside in a HUBZone
- [ ] Employees work 40+ hours in the 4-week period

### Documents:
- [ ] Lease/deed proving office is in HUBZone (lease started 30+ days before review)
- [ ] Photos or virtual walk-through of office
- [ ] Employee roster with home addresses
- [ ] Payroll records
- [ ] Articles of Organization, Operating Agreement
- [ ] Membership certificates
- [ ] Business and personal tax returns
- [ ] Active SAM.gov registration

### NOTE: SBA may conduct site visit. Only apply if address confirms HUBZone on the map.
